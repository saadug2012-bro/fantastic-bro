Advantages

    Wi‑Fi offers many advantages such as convenience, mobility, and ease of installation. Users can move freely without needing physical cables. It also supports multiple devices at the same time.

Disadvantages

    Wi‑Fi networks can face security risks if not protected properly. Hackers can access unsecured networks. Signal interference and limited range may also reduce performance.

Security & Future

To improve safety, Wi‑Fi networks use encryption methods like WPA2 and WPA3. In the UAE, public and private networks are secured to protect user data and privacy.The future of Wi‑Fi in the UAE is very advanced. The country is investing in smart cities, Wi‑Fi 6 and Wi‑Fi 7 technologies, and nationwide high‑speed connectivity to support innovation and digital growth.
